Italian Bruschetta Mix

Description

This Italian bruschetta mix is a great choice for a party appetizer! I like to have this on toasted ciabatta buns, with mozzarella cheese melted on top. Some choose to drizzle balsamic vinegar on top.

Ingredients

  • 8 large roma tomatoes
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp basil
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp parsley flakes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Dice tomatoes and onions into 1x1 mm cubes, put into bowl
  2. Add garlic powder, basil, oregano, parsley flakes, and olive oil to bowl
  3. Mix ingredients together
  4. Your bruschetta mix is now ready. You can serve this on toast
